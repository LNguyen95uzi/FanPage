Post Malone Tribute Page!

Bio

Post Malone, born Austin Richard Post on July 4, 1995, is an American rapper, singer and songwriter.

He has grown popular today in modern hip hop and pop culture due to his blending of hip hop, pop, trap, and rock!

His debut came when he received recognition in 2015 following the debut of his single "White Iverson."

He currently has a recording contract with Republic Records.
